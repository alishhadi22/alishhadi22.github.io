ROS 2 Resolved-Rate Motion Controller for Trajectory Tracking

Jan 2026 · Solo

Resolved-rate motion controller for a 3-DOF spatial robot arm tracking Cartesian trajectories in ROS 2. Derived the analytical Jacobian and implemented Damped Least Squares for singularity robustness; optimized velocity profiles to cut steady-state tracking error from 0.78 mm to 0.08 mm, validated in RViz2.
